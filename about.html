Who We Are

About ZedLearn Online

Zedlearn is an educational platform built by Mutale TV to make quality education for every student

Our Story

Built For Every Zambian Student

ZedLearn Online was created with one simple belief that every Zambian student deserves access to quality educational resources, regardless of where they live or how much money their family has.

We saw students struggling to find educational resources such as good quality lesson notes and study materials. We decided to build a platform that brings everything together in one place.

Education For All

Every resource on ZedLearn is accessible to some extent and No registration required.

Our Core Values

Accessible
