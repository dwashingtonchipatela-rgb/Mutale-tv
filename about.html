Who We Are

About ZedLearn Online

Zambia's national educational platform — built by Mutale TV to make quality education free for every student

Our Story

Built For Every Zambian Student

ZedLearn Online was created with one simple belief — every Zambian student deserves access to quality educational resources, regardless of where they live or how much money their family has.

We saw students struggling to find ECZ past papers, lesson notes and study materials. We decided to build a platform that brings everything together in one place — completely free, forever.

Start Learning

Free Education For All

Every resource on ZedLearn Online is completely free. No registration required. No hidden fees. Ever.

Our Mission

Bridging The Education Gap

Zambia has incredible students full of potential. Our mission is to make sure every one of them has the tools they need to succeed — from Grade 1 all the way to A-Level.

We cover both old and new ECZ syllabuses so no student is left behind, regardless of which curriculum their school follows.

Our Vision

Proudly Zambian

Built specifically for the Zambian curriculum. ECZ aligned. Made by Zambians for Zambians.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Always Free

Every resource on ZedLearn Online is and will always be free for all Zambian students.

ECZ Aligned

All content is aligned to the official Zambian ECZ curriculum — both old and new syllabuses.

Safe and Secure

A safe environment for students of all ages. Child safety is our top priority.

Mobile First

Designed to work perfectly on any phone — because most Zambian students browse on mobile.

National Reach

Serving students from Livingstone to Kasama — every corner of Zambia covered.

Quality Content

All resources are reviewed for quality and accuracy before being published.
