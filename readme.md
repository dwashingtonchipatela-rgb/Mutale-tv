# ZedLearn Online

**Zambia's National Educational Platform**

A product of Mutale TV — providing free educational resources for Zambian students from Grade 1 to A-Level.

## What We Offer

- ECZ Past Papers — Grade 7, 9 and 12
- Lesson Scripts — All subjects
- Educational and Fiction Books
- Video Lessons via ZedLearn Academy on YouTube
- Student Community — WhatsApp, Telegram, Google Meet

## Tech Stack

- HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Supabase — Database and Authentication
- Netlify — Hosting and Deployment
- Google Analytics — Visitor Tracking
