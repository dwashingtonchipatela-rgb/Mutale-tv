Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy was last updated in January 2026 and applies to all users of ZedLearn Online (zedlearnonline.co.zm), a product of Mutale TV.

1. Who We Are

ZedLearn Online is a free educational platform operated by Mutale TV, a Zambian media and education company. Our website is at zedlearnonline.co.zm. When we say "we", "us" or "our" we mean Mutale TV and ZedLearn Online. When we say "you" we mean any person visiting or using ZedLearn Online.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service. This includes:

  • Names submitted through our contact or review forms
  • Email addresses submitted through our contact form
  • Reviews and comments you choose to submit publicly
  • General usage data collected through Google Analytics — which pages are visited, how long visits last, what devices are used
  • Cookie data — small files stored on your device to remember your preferences

We do NOT collect passwords, payment information, national ID numbers or any other sensitive personal data. You do not need to create an account to use ZedLearn Online.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to respond to messages you send through our contact form, display approved reviews publicly on the platform, understand how students use ZedLearn Online so we can improve it, and remember your cookie preferences.

4. Third Party Services We Use

ZedLearn Online uses the following trusted third party services which may process your data:

  • Google Analytics — tracks general visitor statistics
  • Google Fonts — loads our website fonts
  • Supabase — our secure database that stores reviews and contact submissions
  • Netlify — our website hosting provider
  • YouTube — embedded video lessons
  • Google Drive — file hosting for downloadable resources

5. Children's Privacy

ZedLearn Online serves students including children under the age of 13. We take children's privacy extremely seriously. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. Our review system requires age acknowledgement before submission. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information to us please contact us at admin@zedlearnonline.co.zm and we will delete it promptly.

6. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. ZedLearn Online uses cookies to remember your cookie consent preference and to understand site traffic through Google Analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data including encrypted HTTPS connections, secure database storage through Supabase, and input sanitization to prevent malicious data from being stored.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to any personal data we hold about you, request correction of incorrect data, request deletion of your data, and object to how we use your data. To exercise any of these rights please contact us at admin@zedlearnonline.co.zm.

9. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of ZedLearn Online after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy please contact us at admin@zedlearnonline.co.zm.
