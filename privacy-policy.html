Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy was last updated in April 2026 and applies to all users of ZedLearn Online, a product of Mutale TV.

1. Who We Are

ZedLearn Online is a an educational platform operated by Mutale TV. Our website is zedlearnonline.co.zm.(Domian name will be obtained) When we say "we" we mean Mutale TV and ZedLearn Online.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary. This includes names and emails submitted through our contact or review forms, reviews you choose to submit, and general usage data through Google Analytics.

We do NOT collect passwords, payment information or national ID numbers. You do not need to create an account to use ZedLearn Online.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information to respond to messages, display approved reviews, understand how students use ZedLearn Online, and remember your cookie preferences.

4. Third Party Services

  • Google Analytics — tracks general visitor statistics
  • Google Fonts — loads website fonts
  • Supabase — secure database for reviews and submissions
  • Netlify / GitHub Pages — website hosting
  • YouTube — embedded video lessons
  • Google Drive — file hosting for downloadable resources

5. Children's Privacy

ZedLearn Online serves students including children under 13. We take children's privacy extremely seriously. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information please contact us at using the contact page

6. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your cookie consent preference and to understand site traffic through Google Analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction or deletion of your data. Contact us using the contact page

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy please contact us using the contact page.
